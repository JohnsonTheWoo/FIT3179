Best and Worst Countries in Life Expectancy


Taking a detailed look at the countries with highest and lowest life expectancy, we can notice some trends. Countries with the highest life expectancy are first-world countries, while countries with the lowest life expectancy are third-world countries often overlooked by the media.

Factors Related to Life Expectancy



According to our analysis, Number of Physicans per thousand was determined as the most important factor that critically infleunces the life expectancy. This is mainly due to the fact that in third world countries with limited access to doctors, it is likely for a small problem to develop into a life threatening problem. Moreover early treatment of time limited problems such as cancer would be impossible
