Best and Worst countries in life expectancy

Taking a detailed look of the countries with highest and lowest life expectancy, we can notice some trend here. Factors related with the life expectancy

As you can see here, there is a limited correlationship between the GDP and Life expectancy. GDP and life expectancy generally show a positive correlation, with wealthier nations often having longer life expectancies due to better healthcare and living conditions. However, many factors beyond GDP, such as healthcare access and lifestyle choices, also influence life expectancy. It's a complex interplay of economic, social, and healthcare factors.
