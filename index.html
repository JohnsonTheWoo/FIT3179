Best and Worst Countries in Life Expectancy

Taking a detailed look at the countries with highest and lowest life expectancy, we can notice some trends. Countries with the highest life expectancy are first-world countries, while countries with the lowest life expectancy are third-world countries often overlooked by the media.

Factors Related to Life Expectancy

There is a limited correlation between GDP and life expectancy. While wealthier nations tend to have longer life expectancies due to better healthcare and living conditions, factors like healthcare access and lifestyle choices also play a significant role. It's a complex interplay of economic, social, and healthcare factors.
